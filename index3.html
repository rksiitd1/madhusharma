About Me Section

I am Madhu Sharma, living in South Delhi, holding a diploma in commercial art and an MA in Drawing and Painting from Gwalior. My artistic journey spans over a decade, including teaching at Central School and various private institutions. My passion for art, education, and environmental stewardship drives me to create, inspire, and make a difference.
